# Welcome to Quill

Quill renders **Markdown as it streams** from LLMs. The rendered view updates with each chunk, avoiding the layout thrash typical of generic renderers.

This scenario is *intentionally simple* — a heading, two paragraphs, and a bit of inline `code`. Pick a richer scenario from the config screen to see more.

## What to try next

Switch to **Code walkthrough** to see syntax highlighting in action, or **Kitchen sink** for a full feature tour. The **Advanced** section on the config screen lets you tweak the streaming mode and chunk delay to feel how Quill reacts to different delivery cadences.

Streaming behavior adapts to the selected preset: `balanced` keeps reveal smooth, `snappy` prioritizes responsiveness, and `longForm` trades latency for flow stability on larger documents.
